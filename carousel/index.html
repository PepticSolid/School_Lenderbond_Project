How LenderBond can help you get ahead

What is LenderBond?

LenderBond is a website where loan companies can post their available loans and users can search for loans and find the best loan that fits their needs.

I am a user. How do I start searching for loans?

If you haven't yet, create a standard account and login in. Then go to the loan page and you can search by loan type, APR, or loan amount.

I am a loan company. How do I post a loan?

If you haven't yet, create a buisness account and login in. Then go to create a loan page and enter in the required loan info and submit.

Most Popular Loan Packages

Currently, the three main loan types you can search for are Standard Business Loans, Business Capital Loans, or Startup Business Loans. Each APR, loan term, and loan amount are diffrent for each loan so if a loan dosen't fit your needs keep search because there are many loans available.
